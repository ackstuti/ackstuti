The Institute's operations and management are guided by the following parameters:


AIM


To impart knowledge and life skills to trainees using modern technology, research and consultancy while keeping with changes in the global market.

 

OBJECTIVE


To be a centre of excellence for quality Technical, Industrial, Vocational and Entrepreneurial Training (TIVET) for National development.

 

VISION


To equip, develop and strengthen human resource that will stimulate the commitment to improve the quality of life based on the Christian foundations and elements.

 

MISSION


To provide sound technical exposure which will make this institute a centre of excellence.

CORE VALUES


  1. Project a positive image of the Institute in the society.
  2. Uphold high standards of discipline among students, teaching and supporting staff.
  3. Encourage gender equality in enrolment in all courses offered at the Institute.
  4. Observe prudence in the management of the institutional resources.
  5. Avail equal opportunities to each member of the community to realize their potentials.
  6. Promote creativity, innovative and participative approach in all activities.
  7. Uphold honesty and truthfulness.

 

AIMS FOR PROFESSIONAL TRAINING PROGRAMMES


  1. Provide training opportunities for the increasing number of school leavers to enable them to be self-supporting
  2. Develop practical skills and attitudes which will lead to income generating activities in the urban and rural areas through self-employment
  3. Provide practical education and training skills which are responsive and relevant to Kenya's agricultural, industrial, commercial and economic needs
  4. Provide the technical knowledge and vocational skills necessary to enhance the pace of the nation's development
  5. Encourage self-employment while at the same time producing skilled artisans, technicians and technologists for both formal and informal sectors

 

AIMS FOR THE CRAFT TRAINING PROGRAMMES


  1. Develop skills which will be responsive and relevant to the country's human resources required at the middle level
  2. Prepare the trainees so that they can enter the world of work with confidence for either salaried employment or self-employment
  3. Impart adequate skills which will enable the trainee to operate either as a craftsman or perform middle supervisory functions
