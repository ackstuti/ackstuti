Student Enrolment

At St Thomas Ufanisi Training Institute, student enrolment takes place several times during an academic year. We also cater for those who fail to get enrolled for various reasons and ensure they do so during the following intake. We are also flexible enough to cater for those who wish to study at other times than the normal study hours. Please come by our offices or contact us using the contact details at our contacts page here.

New Library Facility

A new library facility has been set up to cater for the various teaching and learning needs of both staff and students. This will ensure that both groups acquire the knowledge they need not just to teach or pass examinations, but to become productive and exemplary individuals within the community. The facility is still being expanded.
