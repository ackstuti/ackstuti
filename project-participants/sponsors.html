The Institute's sources of funds include fees income, sales from Production Unit (Tailoring and Dressmaking, and Welding and Carpentry units), grants from support partners and overseer churches, etc.

The Institute's sponsors and support partners include:


SPONSOR

1. ACK ST THOMAS CHURCH AND THE DIOCESE OF MOMBASA

The Institute is owned by the ACK St Thomas Church working under the umbrella of the Diocese of Mombasa. They have provided the land on which the Institute is built upon. They oversee the overall management of the institution, for instance by appointing the Institute's Management team and providing guidance on the running of the institution.


SUPPORT PARTNERS

2. ST MARY'S CHURCH OF EVERTON, ENGLAND

   - The church has been instrumental for ensuring the continous growth of the Institute through provision of development  funding, e.g. electricity installation, purchase of computers and computer accessories, computer desks, library facility development, funds to design this website, among others.


3. MR BRIAN AND MRS MARGARET OF ENGLAND

   - The Institute is blessed to have Mr Brian and Mrs Margaret who have volunteered to help tirelessly in the stabilization and expansion of the Institute by providing salary grants for the payment of one Tailoring instructor's salary.

 

4. THE KENYA GOVERNMENT, THROUGH LOCAL AUTHORITIES (through student bursary provision)

   - The government, through its local authority (Kilifi County Council), CDF, etc, has assisted the institution by ensuring that needy students get the financial support (bursaries) they need to enrol in the various courses offered.


5. COMMUNITY MEMBERS

   - The community members have also played a large role in the growth of the institution by providing students who enrol in the various courses offered.
