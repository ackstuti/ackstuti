The ACK St Thomas Church Ufanisi Training Institute is owned by the ACK St Thomas church and the Diocese of Mombasa.

The project was initiated to cater for the educational needs of people at the local level, by offering training in various disciplines, without racial or religious discrimination while at the same time maintaining its foundational Christian values, ethics and traditions.
